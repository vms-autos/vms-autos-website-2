Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer finance?

Yes — we work with trusted lenders to offer flexible HP/PCP options (subject to status). We’ll talk through monthly payments and deposit choices to keep things clear.

Can I part-exchange my current car?

Absolutely. Share your reg, mileage and a few photos and we’ll provide a fair valuation. You can use it against any vehicle in stock.

Can I reserve a car?

Yes — a small refundable holding deposit removes the car from sale while we arrange viewing and paperwork.

Do you take cars on sale-or-return?

We do. We handle marketing, enquiries and viewings, and transfer the funds to you once sold.

What checks do you perform?

Each vehicle is inspected and road-tested. History and mileage checks are performed for your peace of mind.

Where are you based? Do you offer delivery?

We’re UK-based and can arrange nationwide delivery at cost. Get in touch for a tailored quote.

What is VMS Autocare?

Our mobile aftercare arm — detailing, vehicle security, diagnostics and coding to keep your car looking sharp and running right.
