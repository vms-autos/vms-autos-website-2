How We Work

  • Consult & Quote. We understand your goals and vehicle, then recommend a tailored package or product set.
  • Preparation. We protect surfaces, plan cable runs, and set expectations clearly.
  • Execution. Methodical detailing, tidy installs, or thorough diagnostics & coding — done carefully and cleanly.
  • Quality Check. Final inspection, tracker setup, dashcam test footage, paint inspection and wipe-downs.
  • Handover & Tips. We demo your setup and explain maintenance/aftercare clearly.
