Our Services

Detailing

Restore & protect. Safe wash methods (snow-foam prewash, two-bucket contact wash), full decontamination (tar/iron removal & clay), and machine polishing to correct light defects and revive gloss. Finish with durable sealants or ceramic coatings. Interiors deep-cleaned with attention to touchpoints and materials.

Why it matters: your car stays cleaner for longer, looks incredible, and holds value better. Our 4 packages scale to the condition and goals for your vehicle.

Vehicle Security

Prevent, deter, recover. Modern theft targets keyless systems. We supply and install Thatcham-approved trackers (with app alerts & recovery), immobilisers, alarms, and discreet dashcams — all fitted with OEM-style wiring routes and tidy finishes.

Why it matters: reduced risk, lower downtime, potential insurance benefits, and peace of mind. We specify hardware to suit your vehicle and use case.

Explore security options →

Diagnostics & Coding

Clarity over guesswork. Dealer-level OBD diagnostics to read fault codes, live data and module status. We verify fixes, reset where appropriate, and provide a simple report. OEM-style coding can enable supported convenience features (e.g., mirror fold on lock, DRL behaviour, welcome lights).

Why it matters: catch issues early, avoid unnecessary parts, and tailor your car to your preferences — safely and reversibly.
