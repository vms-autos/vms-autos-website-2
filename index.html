VMS Autocare

Our mobile aftercare division. We come to you with meticulous detailing, professional security installations, and dealer-level diagnostics & coding.

Detailing

Enhance & protect. Safe pre-wash, two-bucket contact wash, tar/iron decon and clay remove bonded grime. Single or multi-stage machine polish revives gloss; durable sealants or ceramics lock in the finish. Interior deep cleans available. Choose from Refresh, Enhancement, Protection or New-Car Ceramic packages.

Vehicle Security

Prevent. Deter. Recover. Keyless theft is rising — trackers (with app alerts & recovery), immobilisers and alarms reduce risk dramatically. Dashcams provide evidence and peace of mind. We specify hardware to suit your vehicle and install neatly with OEM-style routing, then show you how everything works.

Diagnostics & Coding

Clarity over guesswork. Dealer-grade tools read live data and fault codes to identify issues accurately. We can reset after repairs and provide a clear report. OEM-style coding unlocks supported features (e.g., mirror fold on lock, DRL behaviour, comfort indicators). Always safe and reversible.
